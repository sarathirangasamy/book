    Sri Central Silks

    Our dreams unfolded when
    our Founder Mr.A.K.Palanisamy Gounder set out on a noble journey in 1976 with a unit on Sankari Road in Tiruchengode.

Dreams are meant to be realised and lived.

Again in 1985, we opened Mathiarasu Central Silk Showroom in North Car Street. Then in 2007 our showroom got a facelift when it was renovated and rechristened by the successor Mr.P. Mathiarasu as Sri Central Silks.

Besides, we have our own looms wherein we make white shirts in the brand name ‘Central’. We also make cotton and silk sarees and market throughout Tamil Nadu. In spite of achieving a remarkable name and considerable foot presence in the textile industry, we continuously crave to sustain the reputation we have built earnestly over the years.
